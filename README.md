# Ecommerce-analysis-tableau
E-commerce sales analysis uncovering customer behavior, product trends, and revenue insights
# Overview 
This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset to uncover key business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends.
# Key Insights
Strong revenue growth with seasonal peaks toward the end of the year
High dependence on the United Kingdom market
Clear difference between high-volume and high-revenue products
Customer base follows a Pareto distribution
Significant number of customers at risk (RFM analysis)
# Tool Used
Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
Tableau
# Analysis Performed
Data cleaning and preprocessing
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Customer segmentation (RFM)
Business insights generation
# Outcome
Developed an interactive dashboard to support business decision-making and identify opportunities in customer retention and product strategy.
